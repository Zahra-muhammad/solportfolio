Born

I grew up noticing everything — the weight of a typeface, a misplaced pixel, the friction of a button placed slightly wrong.

I studied Creative Computing at Bath Spa University — to understand how sensation is engineered.

Based in the UAE, working globally across web, illustration, mobile, and interactive.
